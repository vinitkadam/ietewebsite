Techfest

WHAT IS A TECHFEST, YOU ASK?

It is a platform for budding engineers to shed their inhibitions they have about their technical prowess by participating & organizing several national level technical events, technical paper presentations (TPP). You can present a paper on a specific technology, or you can take it a step ahead, create a piece of tech f your own design in a national level project competition. There always needs to be balance in an engineer’s life, so techfests not only help you brush up on your organizational skills but also make sure you have ample amount of fun. Fun, engaging games with a technical twist, is how we define it.
