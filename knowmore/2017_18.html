Workshops

ENHANCING ANALTICAL SKILLS IN ELECTRONIC DEVICES & AMPLIFICATION CIRCUITS USING SIMULATION

PSPICE (Simulation Program for Integrated Circuits Emphasis) is a general purpose analog circuit simulator that is used to verify circuit designs and predict the circuit behaviour. PSPICE is a PC version of SPICE which has analog and digital libraries of standard components which makes it a useful too for a wide range of analog and digital communcations. PSPICE workshop was conducted by IETE-RAIT. This workshop was organized on 16th & 17th September, 2017

VHDL WORKSHOP

IETE-RAIT organized the VHDL workshop on 23rd and 24th September,2017. This workshop was organized for SE students and essentially provided them with an overview of the applications of VHDL (VHSIC Hardware Description Language) in electronic design automation.

GET STARTED WITH ARDUINO
