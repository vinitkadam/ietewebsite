IETE RAIT YEAR 2015-2016

Workshops

PCB DESIGNING

A PCB designing workshop was conducted by the technical members of our committee for the third year students of the department. The aim of this workshop was to make the students learn the designing and manufacturing of a printed circuit board using open source EAGLE and PCB Wizard design software and with various active and passive components such as Regulators, Diodes, Resistors, Capacitors, Inductors, Switches, etc. 100 members from TEs attended the workshop.

RASPBERRY PI

we organised Raspberry Pi workshop in the department from 10th August 2015 to 14th August 2015. This workshop was organised for Third year students. 100 students showed their interest towards this technology.

Techfest

IGNITE 2015

IGNITE 2015 was scheduled on 1st, 2nd and 3rd October 2015. The festival witnessed many technical events like Electromania, Campus Calling. Wings of Fire: The theme for the festival was inspired by the book written by the Rocket Man of India- Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam.

HERTZ 2016

With the persistent aim of creating a platform to compete, learn and promote the intricacies of technology and science, the annual technical festival Hertz was held for the even semester of the year 2016. It was scheduled on 18th to 20th March 2016.

Magazine

SPECTRA 16

The annual magazine Spectra for the year 2016 included several articles on several trending topics. The magazine included an introduction to the concept of 4G, its advantages and disadvantages. Also, an insight to the commonly encountered topic of conceptual time travelling and the paradoxes related to it.
