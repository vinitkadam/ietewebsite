IETE RAIT YEAR 2016-2017

Workshops

GET STARTED WITH ARDUINO

IETE-RAIT organized its first Arduino workshop on 23rd and 24th September 2016. It was a 2 days workshop for TE-EXTC students. This two days Arduino workshop was organized for 70 students. Various topics of the workshop are ultrasonic sensor, DHT11, LCD, Seven segment display, DC motor.

Techfest

IGNITE 2016

IGNITE 2016 was scheduled on 29th September to 1st October 2016. Theme for the festival was ‘Vintage’.

Magazine

SPECTRA 17

Spectra 17 glorifies the concept of technology as an untapped and endless resource. The first article talks all about the evolution of 5G, its surprising potentials in the real world, and its possibility in the future. The next article deals with data as a huge reservoir of information which can be effectively used to make life more organized, namely Big Data. And many more articles.
